// Products

Five products covering the full maritime security stack — from OSINT reconnaissance through edge data collection to crew mobile tools. Built by maritime researchers, deployed on active vessels.

shipcrawler
See what hackers already know about your fleet. Searches Shodan, AIS databases, and public web sources — the same tools attackers use. Produces plain-language reports showing what's exposed, how it could be exploited, and what to fix first.
  • Free 3-vessel scan — no installation, no credentials, no commitment
  • Published, peer-reviewed methodology (IEEE Access 2026)
  • 24-hour turnaround, plain-language reports for fleet managers
  • Volume pricing available for fleet-wide assessments
project haris
Ship-to-shore security and data collection platform designed for the reality of maritime operations — intermittent satellite links, limited bandwidth, no IT crew onboard. Collects navigation, engine, and weather data from sensors you already have.
  • Under $800 hardware — no recurring licence fees
  • Stores weeks of data locally during satellite dropouts
  • Critical alerts prioritised even on slow links
  • Ansible-based automated deployment — one playbook provisions everything
  • Deployed and proven on active commercial vessels
m-soc / monitoring
A security operations centre that understands how ships work. Detects GPS spoofing, VSAT intrusion, navigation system anomalies — threats generic SOCs miss. Three peer-reviewed academic papers back every detection rule.
  • Adds maritime intelligence on top of your existing SIEM — no rip-and-replace
  • Fleet-wide visibility on a single dashboard
  • Automated alert pipeline with plain-language incident reports
  • Deploy on your infrastructure or ours — operational in hours
watchtower
One command to secure your entire fleet. Ansible-based automation provisions firewalls, encrypted connections, monitoring, and time sync identically across every vessel. Full audit trail for PSC and insurance evidence.
  • Identical deployment every time — no variance between vessels
  • Full audit trail — prove what was configured and when
  • Secrets encrypted at rest — never stored in plain text
  • Scales to any fleet size — adding vessel 20 takes the same time as vessel 1
haris crew app
Live vessel data and security alerts on Android phones and tablets. Clean gauges show navigation and engine data. Security alerts from shore appear instantly. No passwords to remember — the phone authenticates automatically through the ship's secure network.
  • No training needed — familiar mobile interface
  • No passwords to manage — automatic authentication
  • Works offline with cached data during satellite dropouts
  • Built with Flutter — modern, fast, extendable
