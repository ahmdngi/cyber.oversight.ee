# Oversight — Maritime & OT Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity consultancy and managed infrastructure for maritime, industrial, and operational technology environments.

## Products

- Shipcrawler — vessel OSINT investigation platform. MMSI/IMO lookup, AIS tracking, port call history, Shodan scanning, Equasis, red-team playbooks.
- Haris — vessel edge data collection and transmission (NMEA 0183/2000, Signal K, compression, webhook/sync).
- M-SOC — Maritime Security Operations Center — monitoring and alerting for vessel fleets.
- Watchtower — vessel edge device management and monitoring.
- Haris Crew App — Flutter-based crew management application.

## Tools

- VSAT Honeypot — maritime satellite communication honeypot.
- Tailscale Topography — Tailscale network topology mapping.
- Agent Eye — Chrome extension for vessel tracking.
- ExtSentry — external perimeter monitoring.
