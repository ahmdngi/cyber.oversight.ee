//Architecture

Every deployment follows a three-layer model designed for the reality of maritime operations — intermittent satellite links, no IT crew onboard, and the need for shore teams to see what's happening.
Edge
rugged hardware on the vessel
connects to existing sensors
stores weeks of data locally
→ syncs when satellite allows
Core
shoreside processing
fleet-wide visibility
threat detection & correlation
→ integrates with your existing tools
Operations
24/7 monitoring
automated alert pipeline
plain-language incident reports
→ actionable, not theoretical
Mesh
encrypted zero-trust network
direct or relayed connections
live topology visibility
→ works through any link
