//Capabilities

  • security/consulting Architecture review, threat modelling, compliance readiness (NIST, ISO 27001). Hands-on implementation, not slide decks.
  • soc/monitoring ELK-based security operations — log ingestion, custom correlation rules, alerting pipelines, and Kibana dashboards deployed on your infrastructure or ours.
  • edge/infrastructure Hardened edge devices for constrained environments. NMEA, CAN bus, industrial sensor collection — encrypted, authenticated, transmitted.
  • network/topology Real-time mesh network visualisation. Tailscale-based topology with direct/relay detection, live node status, and automated alerting.
  • deception/research Honeypot deployment for maritime and industrial OT environments. VSAT emulation, credential capture, attacker behaviour analysis.
  • automation/iac Ansible-based provisioning, firewalld hardening, systemd lockdown, immutable infrastructure. Reproducible, auditable, version-controlled.
